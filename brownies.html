Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1½ cups semisweet chocolate chip

Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 350°F and melt butter.
  2. Mix Base: Whisk cocoa powder, sugar, and espresso into butter.
  3. Add Wet: Mix eggs, vanilla, and salt until glossy.
  4. Incorporate Chocolate: Fold chopped chocolate and flour into batter.
  5. Bake: Spread batter into lined pan and bake 35–40 minutes.
  6. Finish: Cool completely, slice, and serve.

Highlights

  • Bloom cocoa powder in butter for rich flavor.
  • Use semi-sweet or bittersweet chocolate chunks.
  • Crackly top forms when sugar is whisked thoroughly.
