Fresh Garden Salad

Instructions

  1. Chop all ingredients to preferred size.
  2. Add lettuce, cucumber, onion, tomato, bell pepper, and carrots.
  3. Add boiled eggs and optional protein like chicken or tuna.
  4. Toss salad together and add dressing.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Tomato
  • Bell pepper
  • Carrots
  • Boiled eggs
  • Optional protein
  • Italian dressing
