Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 head of lettuce
  • ½ yellow or red onion
  • 3 halves of tri-color bell peppers
  • 1 roma tomatoe
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 carrots
  • 3 boiled eggs
  • Italian dressing
  • 1 can of tuna (optional)
  • 5 oz of chicken (optional)

Instructions

  1. Chop all ingredients to preferred size.
  2. Add lettuce, cucumber, onion, tomato, bell pepper, and carrots.
  3. Add boiled eggs and optional protein like chicken or tuna.
  4. Toss salad together and add dressing.
