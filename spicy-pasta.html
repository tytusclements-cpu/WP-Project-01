Spicy Pasta

Instructions

  1. Boil Pasta: Heat salted water with oregano and cook pasta until done.
  2. Prepare Sauce: Sauté chopped onion in olive oil until golden, then add garlic and chili flakes.
  3. Combine: Reserve some chili-onion mix and add cooked pasta with pasta water. Stir until thick.
  4. Garnish & Serve: Plate pasta and top with the reserved crisp, parsley, and grated cheese.

Highlights

  • Infused pasta water enhances the pasta flavor.
  • Chili onion crisp garnish adds crunchy texture.
  • Pasta water creates a rich glossy sauce.
