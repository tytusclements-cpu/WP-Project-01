Spicy Pasta

Category: Quick • Dinner

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 2–3 people

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water according to package directions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce and chili flakes, then simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Drain pasta and add it to the sauce.
  5. Toss well and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot with basil or grated cheese.
