Ingredient Substitutes

Explore common ingredient substitutes that can be used in recipes.

Eggs

  • 1 tbsp flaxseed + 3 tbsp water
  • ¼ cup applesauce
  • ¼ mashed banana

Best for binding in baking recipes.

Milk

  • Almond milk (Nutty flavor)
  • Oat milk (Creamy texture)
  • Soy milk (High protein)

Use a 1:1 ratio for most recipes.

Butter

  • Coconut oil
  • Olive oil
  • Mashed avocado

Oil may change the texture of cookies.

Sugar

  • Honey
  • Maple syrup
  • Coconut sugar

Liquids may require less quantity.
