Classic Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add Wet Ingredients: Add milk, egg, and oil. Mix until smooth.
  3. Cook Pancakes: Heat a lightly oiled pan and pour ¼ cup batter.
  4. Flip & Serve: Flip when bubbles form and edges brown.

Highlights

  • Recipe takes about 15 minutes.
  • Creating a well helps smooth batter.
  • Flip when bubbles appear for perfect pancakes.
